# Sample Conversation 1: Booked

Agent: Thanks for calling about available freight. Please share your MC number so I can verify your authority.

Carrier: My MC is 123456.

Agent: Thanks. I found an active carrier profile. Please tell me your origin, destination, equipment type, or the load ID you want.

Carrier: I want Dallas to Atlanta, dry van.

Agent: Load LD001 is a Dry Van run from Dallas, TX to Atlanta, GA. Pickup is 2026-06-25T09:00:00, delivery is 2026-06-26T18:00:00, and the posted rate is $1800.00. Notes: No Hazmat. Are you interested in taking this load at the posted rate?

Carrier: I can do it for 1850.

Agent: Transfer was successful and now you can wrap up the conversation.
